Service Offerings

Right objective: To complete the data analysis chapter (results, findings and conclusions) | Right time: After data collection process has been completed | My primary responsibility: To deliver all the tables, charts and abstracts | Additional/ optional tasks: Complete the entire masterchart, convert general hypothesis statements into statistical hypothesis statements, find out ways to prove/ disprove/ test statistical hypothesis, write data analysis conclusion, write conclusions on individual hypothesis, any other tasks identified during briefing meeting. | Your responsibility: provide some data

Answers to frequently asked questions

  • Procedure for working with me

    First, email me your requirements, relevant material of your research and your contact number. Second, you will have to deposit my standard consultation fee. Third, we will have a meeting to decide a way forward to complete your requirements.

  • What documents do you require from your client before starting a project of PhD thesis at Dehradun ?

    Do share your research details before moving forward: Topic, objectives, sample data and other documents if necessary. Without these it will not be possible to take up any assignment nor would it be possible to provide any consultation.

  • Will you write the complete PhD thesis at Dehradun?

    Specifically for PhD thesis writing at Dehradun queries, scholars do ask us to write the entire thesis. However, we recommend scholars write all other chapters except the data analysis chapter. Outsourcing only the data analysis chapter will not only reduce the costs significantly it will also get the most out of money spent. For any research documentation, data analysis is at its heart and once it is completed every other aspect is rendered easy. Furthermore, as a researcher you will have far less headaches once data analysis is full and final. We can deliver it fast and to satisfaction as per requirements and chosen package.

  • What is the standard price of services ?

The same is deductible from the final bill. Please feel free to ask details and specifics of the package you have chosen.
